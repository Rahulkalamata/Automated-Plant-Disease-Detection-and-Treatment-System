Diseases

Powdery Mildew

This fungal disease causes white powdery spots on leaves and stems. It thrives in dry, warm conditions. Treatment includes neem oil or sulfur-based sprays.


Bacterial Canker

Leaf spot appears as brown or black patches on foliage. It's usually caused by bacteria or fungi. Remove affected leaves and apply a suitable fungicide.


Anthracnose

Blight causes sudden yellowing, browning, and dying of leaves or stems. It spreads rapidly and needs copper-based fungicides for control.
